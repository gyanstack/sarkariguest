About Us

SarkariGuestHouse.com is the first and only website of its kind. With the help of this blog we tried to make available the information of Government accommodations with complete procedure of booking to common people so that they may also take benefits of facilities which are currently available only for Government people. It is a myth that Government accommodations can be booked by Government employees only and with this blog we tried to make general public aware about their rights of booking of government guest houses.

There are only very few Government guest houses which can be booked by only Government employees otherwise most of the accommodations can be booked by all the Indian Citizens. The charges for Government employees and general public may differ, which also we tried to tell this website. view more...
